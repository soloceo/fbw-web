## Why FBW

FBW LLP was built on a shared observation: that the legal profession in the
Greater Toronto Area had grown larger and more specialised, but for many
individuals and business owners, it had also grown harder to simply find a
lawyer who would listen, explain, and stay with them over time.

We started this firm to offer something different. Three founding partners —
Seth Freedman, Perry Benipal, and Greg Wu — each with a distinct practice
and a long list of clients who have followed us across offices and across
the arc of their lives. Together, with our broader team, we cover the
questions that matter most to families, entrepreneurs, and growing
businesses.

## What clients can expect

A partner on every file. Fees you can see and predict. Counsel in the
language you are most comfortable in. And a standing invitation to pick up
the phone when something new comes up — even if it isn't, strictly, a legal
question yet.

We look forward to working with you.
