## Overview

We represent businesses and individuals in disputes that touch the core of
their commercial lives. Most of our litigation practice is focused on the
Superior Court of Justice and the Ontario Court of Appeal, with a parallel
commitment to alternative dispute resolution where it serves the client's
interests.

## Our approach

Litigation is expensive, disruptive, and frequently avoidable. Our first
question is always strategic: what is the client actually trying to
achieve, how long can they afford to wait, and what set of outcomes would
count as "good enough"? From there we build the case — aggressively when
the facts call for it, patiently when the better play is to wait.
