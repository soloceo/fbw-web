## Overview

Real estate is one of the largest financial decisions most individuals and
businesses will make. Our real estate practice is organised around two
complementary ideas: that every transaction deserves the same rigour
regardless of dollar value, and that clear, plain‑language communication
is as important as technical execution.

We act for individual buyers and sellers, investors, developers, lenders,
landlords, and tenants across the Greater Toronto Area — with particular
depth in residential conveyancing, small commercial acquisitions, and
leasing for owner‑operated businesses.

## How we work

Every real estate file at FBW LLP is supervised by a partner from the moment
the agreement is signed through to closing. We coordinate with your agent,
lender, and any third‑party professionals (surveyors, home inspectors,
title insurers) so that you have a single point of contact — and a single
unambiguous next step — at every stage.

## Starting a matter

If you have a signed agreement of purchase and sale, a draft commercial
lease, or simply a question about a transaction you are considering, we are
happy to provide an initial assessment at no charge.
