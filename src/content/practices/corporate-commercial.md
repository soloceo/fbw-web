## Overview

Most of our corporate clients are privately held and closely managed —
professional corporations, family businesses, technology companies, real
estate holding structures, and the operating entities of entrepreneurs
building their first venture. We advise them through the decisions that
shape a business over the long run: how to incorporate, how to bring in
partners or investors, how to paper key relationships, and ultimately how
to exit.

## What sets us apart

We favour long‑term relationships over transactional volume. That means
partners who remember the contour of your business from one matter to the
next, advice calibrated to the actual stakes in front of you, and a
willingness to tell you — directly — when the right answer is "don't do
this deal."
