## Overview

An estate plan is, in the end, a letter to the people you love. Our wills
and estates practice combines technical precision with the time and care
the subject deserves. We draft wills, powers of attorney, and trust
arrangements that are clear to the people who will one day have to read
them — and we support those same families through administration when the
time comes.

## For business owners

We work frequently with business owners whose most valuable asset is also
their most complicated. Succession planning, freeze structures,
shareholders' agreements that dovetail with the will — these matter as
much as the will itself, and we coordinate them as one plan.
