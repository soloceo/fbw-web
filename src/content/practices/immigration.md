## Overview

Our immigration practice serves individuals and families navigating
Canadian immigration law, as well as Ontario employers who need to hire
internationally. We guide clients through Express Entry, provincial
nomination, family sponsorship, work and study permits, and humanitarian
applications — combining careful file preparation with honest assessment
of what is realistic given each client's circumstances.

## Why clients choose us

Immigration matters are rarely *only* immigration matters. A new permanent
resident may also need a real estate lawyer, an accountant, and a will.
Our ability to coordinate across those questions — in English, Punjabi,
Mandarin, Hindi, and Cantonese — is part of what clients come to us for.
