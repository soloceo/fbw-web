## Overview

Most of our tax work sits inside the other things we do — structuring a
real estate acquisition, papering a shareholders' agreement, planning an
exit, freezing an estate. We advise on the tax dimension of those
transactions in close coordination with our clients' accountants, with a
clear preference for practical structures that the client actually
understands over clever ones that only the lawyer does.

Where a matter requires specialist tax expertise beyond our scope, we
will tell you — and often bring in a trusted external tax adviser on
your behalf.
