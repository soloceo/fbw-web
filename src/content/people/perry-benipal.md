Perry Benipal is a founding partner of FBW LLP, with a practice that bridges
residential and commercial real estate, wills and estate planning, and
immigration law. He serves individuals, families, and small businesses across
the Greater Toronto Area — frequently guiding clients through their first
home purchase, their first business, and their first estate plan as part of
one continuing relationship.

Perry is known for his approachability and his ability to translate dense
legal mechanics into plain language. He is fluent in English, Punjabi, and
Hindi, and works with a large South Asian client base across the GTA —
including from the firm's Oakville and Toronto offices.
