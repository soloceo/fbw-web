Greg Wu is a founding partner of FBW LLP, where his practice centres on
commercial real estate, residential conveyancing, and corporate matters for
privately held clients and entrepreneurs. Drawing on more than a decade of
transactional experience across the Greater Toronto Area, Greg advises on
acquisitions, dispositions, commercial leasing, and the full life cycle of
private companies from formation through to exit.

Clients turn to Greg for his ability to move complex files forward without
losing sight of practical outcomes. He is known for patient, thorough
communication — especially with first‑time buyers, cross‑border investors,
and Mandarin‑speaking business owners.

Greg is fluent in English, Mandarin, and Cantonese, and advises clients from
both the Toronto and Markham offices of FBW LLP.
